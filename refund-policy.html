Return, Refund & Cancellation Policy

← Back to Home

Because our Sattu, Bhuja, spices, and oils are food products, we want to be upfront about how returns and refunds work.

Returns

We don't accept returns once an order has been delivered, as our products are consumable food items.

Refunds

Refunds at Gupta Jee Bhandar are available under the following conditions:

  • If the product you ordered is unavailable and payment has already been made.
  • If the product was damaged in delivery. In this case, we may ask you to share a photo of the damaged product before processing your refund.
  • If the product was lost in transit and the delivery could not be completed.

Cancellations

Gupta Jee Bhandar reserves the right to cancel any order for any reason. We may also request additional information to process your order. If your order, or a portion of it, is cancelled, we will notify you.

If your order is cancelled after your card or UPI payment has already been charged, you will receive a refund within 5–7 working days.

Contact Us

If you'd like to reach out about a return, refund, or cancellation, contact us at orders@guptajeebhandar.com, or visit us at our counter in Ambedkar Market B Block, Bus Stand, Mehboob Khan Tola, Purnia, Bihar 854301.
