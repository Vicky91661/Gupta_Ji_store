The Art Behind Our Roots

Madhubani (Mithila) painting has decorated the walls and courtyards of Bihar for generations — traditionally hand-painted by women using natural pigments, with fish, peacocks, and the sun standing for prosperity and life. We borrow that same visual language, because our food carries the same soil and the same soul.

The Revival Movement

We started with a definitive goal: to change how the youth look at native Bihari food. For generations, Sattu and Bhuja have sustained the hardworking people of Mithila and Seemanchal.
