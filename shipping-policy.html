Domestic Shipping

All orders are processed within 24 hours of being placed with us. We typically dispatch orders the same day or the next day, subject to stock availability. Orders are shipped and delivered every day of the week.

Cost of Shipping

We charge a flat shipping fee of ₹65 on all orders.

Order Confirmation and Tracking

You'll receive a confirmation message as soon as your order has been shipped. If you'd like an update on your order at any time, reach out to us directly through our Contact page and we'll look it up for you.

Damages

We take full responsibility for a safe and comfortable delivery experience. If you receive a damaged package or jar, please take a photo and let us know at orders@guptajeebhandar.com, and we'll send across a replacement.

As for products you've already received in good condition, they're yours to enjoy or share as you please.
